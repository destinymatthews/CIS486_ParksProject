About Alloys Park

Information

Colbert Alloys Park is located on the beautiful Wilson Lake. Open year round. We recently have made some updates and it is more beautiful than ever. It is just far enough away from everything for peace, quite, and relaxation. But, conveniently close to, Brick Hatton Volunteer Fire Dept., the Shell station, Foodland, 100 Proof Grill, Ace Hardware, Dollar General, and Stansfield's Steakhouse. All are less than 3.0 miles down the road. We have a storm shelter on site. We also have a public boat ramp, various fishing piers, a covered lighted pavilion with bathrooms and a fireplace (available to rent), picnic and grilling areas by the water, a playground, adult swings all over, various walking trails, and a heated bath house with hot showers by the camp ground. We welcome all motor home, RV, tent, and hammock campers to come visit our 40+ acres of pure nature.

Rules

① Only one unit designed for camping (including tents) will be allowed to occupy each camp site.


② The park manger must be notified if a campsite will be left unattended for more than 24 hours.


③ No sub-letting of any units designed for camping (including tents) or of the camp site.


④ Only two vehicles will be allowed parking on each camp site. overflow parking is available.


⑤ Parking of vehicles, utility trailers, boats or visitors' vehicles not allowed in unoccupied camp sites.


⑥ Colbert County will NOT be responsible for any damages to campers, motor homes, utility trailers, boats or any visitors' vehicles or property parked at County Parks.


⑦ Pets must be kept on a leash or otherwise restrained. You MUST clean up after your pets.


⑧ All campers are responsible for keeping their camp site in order with no junk or clutter. Garbage must be in proper containers.


⑨ Campfires must be in an approved campfire ring or approved by management and must NOT be left unattended.


⑩ Any camper may be evicted for any significant disturbance without a refund. Public drunkenness or the use of illegal drugs by anyone will result in immediate eviction.


⑪ Campers will be responsible for action and behavior of their children and other guests, and will be responsible for any damages they might cause.


⑫ Quiet time will be 10:00PM - 6:00AM.


⑬ Anyone operating a motorized vehicle within the campgrounds must have a valid driver's license.


⑭ No motorized vehicles allowed on walking trails. (No exceptions)


⑮ Water Leaks: If you have a water connection leaking, please notify the park manager. If the leak is on your end, please fix it.


Regulations

▶ The county parks are for your enjoyment. Please be courteous and respectful of other campers and follow all of the rules/regulations.

▶ All Parks operated by Colbert County will be made available to all members of the general public without distinction or discrimination of race, color, religion, sex, national origin, handicap or age.

▶ Each park operated by Colbert County will employ a full-time Park Manager who will reside on site.

▶ The park manager will be responsible for providing information regarding park rental, availability and scheduling reservation for rental.

▶ All rental fees must be paid in advance. No credit allowed. NO REFUNDS.

▶ The park manager will be responsible for notifying the proper authorities when necessary to maintain safety for campers and to secure the area inside the park boundaries.

▶ The park manager will notify the County Administrator of any notification of authorities necessary for safety and security within the park boundaries
